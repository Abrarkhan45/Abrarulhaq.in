My Skills




Web Development

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications that people access over the Internet. This includes designing the user interface, writing code to make the site interactive, and managing the back-end infrastructure that stores and processes data. Web development includes both the visual aspects that users see and the behind-the-scenes functions that drive a website's functionality. It is a combination of technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, server-side languages, databases, and frameworks. From building simple static websites to complex dynamic applications, web development plays a vital role in shaping the online experiences we encounter every day.

Video Editing

Video editing is the art and process of manipulating and arranging video clips to create cohesive and engaging visual stories. This includes selecting and trimming clips, adding transitions, applying effects, and including audio to improve the overall quality and impact of your video. Video editing is critical to turning raw footage into a polished final product, whether it's a short film, documentary, marketing video or content for a social media platform. Through the skilled use of editing software, professionals and amateurs alike can create videos that captivate audiences, communicate information effectively, and evoke emotion.
